Why Website Maintenance Matters

Websites don't run themselves. The best sites evolve constantly. Most businesses regret skipping maintenance the first time they accidentally break something — or worse, get hacked.

🛡️
Security Threats
Outdated plugins and software are the #1 entry point for hackers. Regular updates close vulnerabilities before attackers exploit them.
⚡
Speed & Performance
Slow websites lose visitors. Maintenance includes performance audits, caching optimisation, and image compression to keep load times fast.
📈
SEO Rankings
Google penalises broken links, slow pages, and outdated content. Consistent upkeep keeps your rankings healthy and climbing.
💼
Business Continuity
Downtime costs money. Regular backups and uptime monitoring ensure your site is always available when customers need you.
🔄
Content Freshness
Stale content signals a dead business to visitors. Regular updates keep your offers, portfolio, and blog current and compelling.
🤝
Customer Trust
A well-maintained site signals professionalism. Broken pages, expired certificates, or slow load times destroy first impressions instantly.
