What Affects the Price of a Website?

Every business is unique. These key factors determine your final cost — understanding them helps you budget smarter.

🆕
New vs Redesign
Fresh designs typically cost more — the designer starts from scratch with no existing brand or structure to reference. Redesigns reuse assets, cutting discovery time.
📄
Number of Pages
More pages mean more design, development, and content work. A 3-page site costs far less than a 25-page corporate portal with custom layouts.
📱
Responsive / Mobile Design
Responsive design adds 20–30% to cost but is essential. Your site must look great and work flawlessly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
📦
Content & CMS
Do you need a blog or CMS? Will you supply all text and images yourself? Content creation and insertion adds Ksh 500–1,000 per page to the cost.
🛒
E-Commerce Features
Shopping carts, M-Pesa/PayPal gateways, product catalogs, and inventory systems significantly increase complexity and cost.
🔍
SEO & Marketing
On-page SEO setup, Google Analytics, email marketing platforms, and social integrations add value — and add to the investment.
